Real-time tactile-equivariant residual correction for robust manipulation.

Abstract

We propose a tactile-equivariant residual correction framework that leverages SO(2) symmetry in surface normal maps to predict angular residuals for real-time yaw correction in contact-rich manipulation. A lightweight equivariant module augments a visuomotor policy at test time, enabling stable in-hand orientation control and robust performance under perturbations.
